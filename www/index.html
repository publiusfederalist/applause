applause

where hands come together.

publish and give applause

publish a post or applaud another and let the world know you agree with the statements.

no signup/login needed

applause authenticates all messages with cryptographic signatures generated from handshake names.

no impersonation

all messages and actions are signed -- even the admins cannot modify your posts.

not a social network

applause does not try to be a social network. you can either agree or disagree. no comments necessary.

transparent actions

all applause are transparently associated with an on-chain handshake name. No stealth votes. It's also open source.

(de)centralized hybrid

applause is a centralized website relying on decentralized identity.
